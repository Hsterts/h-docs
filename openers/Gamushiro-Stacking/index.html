Gamushiro Stacking

Gamushiro Stacking is a Triple-Double T-Spin opener created by 組グッズ (eng. "KMGZ") and ゆう (eng. "Yuu"). This opener grants reliable 8-line Perfect Clear chances or a favorable midgame board without the PC.
Compared to similar popular “Wasted T” openers such as DT-Cannon and BT-Cannon, Gamushiro Stacking boasts numerous advantages:

  • Reliable PC and combo downstack chances.
  • Superior midgame flexibility.
  • Resistance to spikes from other openers.

This opener requires considerable effort to memorize and use in-game to its full potential.

1st Bag

The first bag is always buildable, keeping either L or J in hold depending on which one comes first.

2nd Bag

The following setups are listed by order of their effective priority. Their names are their respective piece dependencies. The LO and JO variants are part of the original opener. The SOJ Alt was discovered by metallicLurker. The OS Alt was discovered by GenDragFan.

The leftover L piece from the first bag is placed in one of two positions. (Excluding OLL.)
