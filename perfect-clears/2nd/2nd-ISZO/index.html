Queue-Based Setups

ISZO’s queue-based setups are practical and can be learned easily. Not counting mirrors, you only need to learn 7 setups to reach 100% cover. Don’t forget that some placements that aren’t in the setup image may still be placed as long as it does not interfere with the necessary pieces.
